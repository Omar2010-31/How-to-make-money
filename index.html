¿What is a business?

A business is an economic activity that involves the purchase, sale or exchange of goods and services with the objective of making a profit. Starting a business requires planning and strategy.

A business has the following key characteristics:

  • Profit generation: Its main objective is to obtain economic benefits.
  • Exchange of goods or services: Offer products or services in exchange for money.
  • Risk: Involves uncertainty and possible losses.
  • Planning: Requires strategy and organization to function effectively.
  • Resources: Uses capital, materials and personnel to operate.
  • Target market: Focuses on a specific group of customers.
  • Competition: Operates in a competitive environment with other similar companies.

Types of business

There are different types of businesses, from small startups to large companies. Some common business types include:

  • Product sales business
  • Service businesses
  • online business
  • Franchises
