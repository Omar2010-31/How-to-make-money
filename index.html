¿What is a business?

A business is an economic activity that involves the purchase, sale or exchange of goods and services with the objective of making a profit. Starting a business requires planning and strategy.

A business has the following key characteristics:

  • Profit generation: Its main objective is to obtain economic benefits.
  • Exchange of goods or services: Offer products or services in exchange for money.
  • Risk: Involves uncertainty and possible losses.
  • Planning: Requires strategy and organization to function effectively.
  • Resources: Uses capital, materials and personnel to operate.
  • Target market: Focuses on a specific group of customers.
  • Competition: Operates in a competitive environment with other similar companies.

Types of business

There are different types of businesses, from small startups to large companies. Some common business types include:

  • Online (Digital) Businesses

    Online businesses are ideal for teenagers because they require low initial investment and can be managed from anywhere.

  • Local or In-Person Businesses

    Despite the popularity of digital platforms, physical businesses can still be a great option for teens.

  • Creative business

    If you have artistic skills, there are plenty of opportunities to earn money doing what you love.

  • Food or Beverage Businesses

    If you love cooking or making delicious treats, food-related businesses can be an excellent choice for teens.

Business ideas

For teenagers, there are many creative ways to make money. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  • Online (Digital) Businesses

    • Online store for personalized products: You could sell products like mugs, T-shirts, or hats with unique designs or memes. Use platforms like Etsy, Redbubble, or Shopify.

    Social media video editing: Many people want to improve their content on platforms like YouTube and TikTok. Offering short, eye-catching video editing services can be a profitable business.

    Social media management for small businesses: Help local businesses improve their social media presence by designing posts, managing comments, and growing their followers.

    Digital photography or illustration store: If you have skills in photography or design, create images and sell usage rights through platforms like Shutterstock or Adobe Stock.

  • Local or In-Person Businesses

    • Gardening or outdoor maintenance services: Help neighbors maintain their gardens by mowing lawns, watering plants, and decorating patios.

    Garage sale organization: Assist others in organizing and selling items they no longer need, earning a commission. You could even set up second-hand sales.

    Technology instructor for older adults: Many older adults need help using smartphones, apps, and computers. You could offer classes or tech assistance.

    Dog walking or pet sitting: Offer pet-walking or sitting services when owners are away. This service is highly sought after in urban areas.

  • Creative business

    • Art classes for kids: If you're good at painting, drawing, or another artistic skill, organize workshops for kids on weekends or during the summer.

    Crafting and jewelry making: Create and sell handmade jewelry. You can offer unique, personalized items using materials like beads, leather, or thread.

    Custom stationery products: Design and sell items like planners, notebooks, and personalized stickers. You could sell them at local markets or online.

    Creative photography sessions: Organize photo sessions for teens with fun or themed props. You can also offer basic photography lessons.

  • Food or Beverage Businesses

    • Homemade snack sales: Make and sell snacks like cookies, brownies, or simple desserts. You can sell them at schools, events, or through social media.

    Catering for small events: If you enjoy cooking, offer catering for small parties or events by preparing snacks, sandwiches, or mini cakes.

    Smoothies or juice deliveries: Prepare healthy smoothies and sell them in your neighborhood or on social media. This type of business works well if you can deliver them fresh and quickly.

    Themed dinners at home: Organize unique, themed dinners at home (for example, an Italian or Japanese cuisine night). Invite a small group of friends or neighbors and charge a fee.
